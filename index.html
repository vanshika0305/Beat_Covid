Steps Of Home-Isolation


  • Clean and disinfect your home regularly.
  • Wear reusable or disposable gloves for routine cleaning and disinfection.
  • If someone is sick, keep a separate bedroom and bathroom for the person who is sick (if possible).
  • Prepare for Transportation in case of emergencies.
  • Avoid having domestic help like maids, sweepers, drivers, cooks etc. at home
  • Avoid having junk food or any meals from outside, prepare healthy meals at home.
  • Santize hands regularly or wash hands with soaps properly frequently.
  • Avoid taking stress and miscommunictaions with family & friends.
Try to make a healthy environment for you & your family!

Diet Recommendation


  • Drink 8–10 cups of water every day.
  • Eat less salt and sugar.
  • Do not overcook vegetables and fruit as this can lead to the loss of important vitamins.
  • Avoid processed meats and processed food, fast food, snack food, fried food, frozen pizza, pies, cookies, margarines and spreads.
  • Avoid eating out.
  • Limit your intake of soft drinks or sodas or fruit juices, fruit juice concentrates and syrups, flavoured milks and yogurt drinks.

Vegetarian

Eat fruits, vegetables, legumes (e.g. lentils, beans), nuts and whole grains (e.g. unprocessed maize, millet, oats, wheat, brown rice or starchy tubers or roots such as potato, yam, taro or cassava)

Non-Vegetarian

Eat Foods from animal sources (e.g. meat, fish, eggs and milk) or white meat (e.g. poultry) and fish.(red meat can be eaten 1−2 times per week, and poultry 2−3 times per week).
